Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Connect the camera and ensure it's working properly.
  2. Connect and test the microphone (make sure it's properly charged, keep the second one from the kit charging).
  3. Open Google Chrome and navigate to: https://206.189.10.46:8080
  4. You will be alerted about an insecure connection. Accept and proceed.
  5. After authorization, navigate to: https://steam228.github.io/labirintosP5js/labirintosFinal
  6. Once the page loads, click in the middle to enter full-screen mode.

  1. Ensure the projector is properly connected and functioning.
  2. Open Google Chrome and navigate to: https://206.189.10.46:8080
  3. You will be alerted about an insecure connection. Accept and proceed.
  4. After authorization, navigate to: https://steam228.github.io/labirintosP5js/labirintosWall
  5. Once the page loads, click in the middle to enter full-screen mode.
  6. Position the projector to display the sentences on the designated wall for the poem.
